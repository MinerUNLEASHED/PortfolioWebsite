AI-Powered Face Mask Detector

Leveraging advanced AI technology to accurately detect face mask compliance, promoting health and safety in real-time.

Details

The Face Mask Detection AI is an advanced computer vision system designed to promote safety and compliance in environments where mask-wearing is critical. Built using YOLOv8, a state-of-the-art object detection model, the system accurately identifies whether individuals are wearing face masks, wearing them incorrectly, or not wearing them at all.

Trained on a diverse dataset to ensure robust performance across various scenarios, the AI delivers real-time detection with high accuracy. It can be seamlessly integrated into camera systems to monitor compliance in public spaces, workplaces, or healthcare facilities.

This project emphasizes efficiency and reliability, making it a valuable tool for enforcing health and safety guidelines. With its powerful AI foundation and real-time capabilities, the Face Mask Detection AI contributes to creating safer, healthier environments during times when mask compliance is essential.

The project's codebase includes components for camera detection and automated alerting via text messages, ensuring timely notifications upon pollution detection.
