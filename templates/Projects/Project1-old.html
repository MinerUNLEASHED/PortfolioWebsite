OceanWatch

Leveraging YOLOv8 to revolutionize marine pollution detection through advanced AI for cleaner and healthier oceans.

Code For Nature Challenge 2024 Winner, Code For Nature Challenge 2024 Infosys AI Award, National Stem Festival 2024 Finalist

Details

OceanWatch is an AI-driven system designed to detect marine pollution in real-time. Utilizing the YOLOv8 object detection model, it identifies pollutants in aquatic environments and promptly alerts designated contacts by sending the last five seconds of footage, facilitating swift response to pollution incidents.

The system operates on portable hardware, specifically a Raspberry Pi paired with a camera, enabling deployment beyond standard computing environments. This setup ensures continuous monitoring and detection of oceanic pollution.

To validate OceanWatch's accuracy, a video-based testing approach was employed, given the lack of direct ocean access. The system's performance in these tests demonstrates its capability to effectively identify pollution sources.

The project's codebase includes components for camera detection and automated alerting via text messages, ensuring timely notifications upon pollution detection.
